The Big Issue, 2024, brand refresh

The objective for the speculative design refresh of The Big Issue is to create a brand that embodies inclusivity and celebrates the inherent self-worth of its vendors. By infusing the brand with a new, modern design aesthetic, the goal is to not only enhance its visual appeal but also make it more accessible and engaging to a broader audience.
